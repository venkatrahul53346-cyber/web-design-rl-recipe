⬡ Octave Bank FinTech · 380 services

Cutting MTTR from 47 minutes to under 6 across a regulated payments stack.

Octave's payments platform spans 380 microservices across two AWS regions and an on-prem mainframe bridge. Before Riverbed, incident triage meant grepping correlation IDs across four log stores. After deploying the open-source agent fleet-wide and connecting their existing OTel collectors, on-call engineers now jump from a Slack alert to the offending span in a single click — including the SQL parameters that the legacy tracer used to redact.
