Footnotes
  1. Span volume is billed on accepted spans after head sampling. Spans dropped by tail sampling on Pro and Enterprise do not count toward volume.
  2. Retention is measured in calendar days from span receipt. Cold archive in S3 / GCS is not subject to the hot retention window and remains queryable through the API.
  3. The query API rate limit is averaged over a 60-second window with burst capacity equal to 2× the steady-state limit.
  4. SOC 2 Type II is available under NDA for accounts on Pro and above. Request via contact.
  5. The Riverbed agent is open source under Apache 2.0 at github.com/riverbed-labs/agent — usable against any OTLP-compatible backend, not just Riverbed.
