What you get

Everything in Pro, plus the controls your security team will ask about.

Enterprise is not a feature flag flip. It is a different deployment topology with isolated infrastructure, longer retention defaults, and contractual guarantees about how your trace data is handled.

Single-tenant control plane

Your query engine, ingestion gateway, and storage volumes run on dedicated hardware. No noisy-neighbor span throttling, no shared Kafka partitions. We give you the kubeconfig if you want to look.

isolation: hard

Bring-your-own-cloud (BYOC)

Riverbed deploys the data plane into your AWS, GCP, or Azure account via Terraform. Span payloads never leave your VPC. We operate the cluster through a tightly scoped IAM role; you keep the encryption keys and audit logs.

terraform-module: v3.2

Long-term retention

Default 90-day hot retention, with cold tiering up to 7 years for compliance windows. Re-hydrate cold spans into queryable storage within 15 minutes.

hot 90d · cold 7y

Custom sampling and PII redaction at the agent

Author Lua or WASM policies that drop, hash, or rewrite span attributes before they leave the host. We ship a policy linter, a CI plugin that simulates policies against recorded traffic, and a fleet rollout system that respects your existing change-management cadence.

wasm · lua · regex

SAML / SCIM and audit log streaming

SSO via Okta, Azure AD, Google Workspace, or any SAML 2.0 IdP. SCIM 2.0 for automated provisioning. Every read, query, and configuration change emits an audit event you can stream into Splunk, Datadog, or an S3 bucket.

SAML 2.0 · SCIM 2.0

Deployment

Five weeks, on average, from kickoff to first production trace.

Every Enterprise engagement follows the same milestones. Your TAM walks the team through each one; nothing is hidden behind a sales motion.

  1. 01

    Architecture review

    We map your services, languages, and current instrumentation. You get a written assessment within 5 business days.

  2. 02

    Terraform apply in your VPC

    Data plane lands in a dedicated subnet. Estimated infra cost is documented before plan-apply.

  3. 03

    Agent rollout in shadow mode

    Spans collected and stored, but no dashboards rewired. Validates sampling policies and ingestion volume against forecasts.

  4. 04

    Cutover and decommission

    Dashboards, alerts, and on-call runbooks migrated. Old backend kept warm for 30 days as insurance.

  5. 05

    First QBR at day 60

    Ingest economics, query latency P95, and a six-month roadmap aligned to your platform initiatives.
