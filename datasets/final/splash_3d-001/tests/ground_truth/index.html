The review

It catches the regression, not the typo.

Linters find unused imports. Pliny finds the bug you shipped six months ago that's about to ship again. Each finding is ranked by blast radius and tied back to the exact prior incident, migration, or contract test that informs it.

When Pliny isn't sure, it says so. When it is, it commits a suggested patch.
