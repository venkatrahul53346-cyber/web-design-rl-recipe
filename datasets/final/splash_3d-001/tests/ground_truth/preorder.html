Reservation FAQ

Things people ask
before holding a seat.

Q.01 Am I being charged when I reserve? +
No card is required to reserve. You'll only be billed after the sandbox period, on the day Pliny posts its first review on a non-test PR. We email you twice before that happens.
Q.02 Why batches? Why not just sign up? +
Each codebase gets its own fine-tune and its own evaluation harness against a sample of historical PRs. We cap onboarding at thirty teams a week so a founding engineer can shepherd each one. It's slower, and we like it that way.
Q.03 Where does my code go? +
Into a single-tenant inference cluster in us-east-1 or eu-west-1, your choice. Code is encrypted at rest with keys you can rotate. Pliny never trains shared models on private repositories — fine-tunes live in your tenant and are deleted on offboard.
Q.04 Can I self-host? +
Workshop tier ships a Helm chart and a sealed weights tarball for your VPC. You'll need one H100 or two L40S for teams up to 80 engineers. We help with the initial install.
Q.05 What if Pliny is wrong? +
Mark the comment with 👎 or drop a reply starting with /pliny disagree and a sentence of context. The next fine-tune cycle (weekly) weights against that pattern. We publish per-team accuracy in your dashboard so you can hold us to it.
Q.06 My team uses Gerrit / Phabricator / a homegrown tool. +
Tell us in the form. We have an experimental Gerrit adapter and a webhook bridge for custom tools. We'll be honest about what works today and what's still a roadmap item.
Q.07 Can I cancel? +
Any time, in one click, from the dashboard. We pro-rate the month. The fine-tune weights for your tenant are deleted within seven days; you get an email confirming the hash.
