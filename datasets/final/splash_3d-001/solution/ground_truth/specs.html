§ 04 — DATA

Boundaries & residency

Your code never trains a shared model. We’ll put that in writing — and in your DPA.

Code retentionEphemeral, 0 days at restSource is decrypted in-memory for the inference span and discarded.
Review artifacts90 days (configurable 7 / 30 / 90)
Training opt-inOff by default. Per-repo toggle. Never on shared base model.
EncryptionTLS 1.3 in transit · AES-256-GCM at rest · per-tenant KMS keys
Residency zonesUS (us-east-2) · EU (eu-west-1, Frankfurt mirror) · APAC (ap-southeast-2)
ComplianceSOC 2 Type II · ISO 27001 · GDPR · CCPA · HIPAA available on Enterprise
Self-hostingAvailable on Enterprise. Kubernetes 1.27+, single-node and HA topologies.

Signal, not noise
