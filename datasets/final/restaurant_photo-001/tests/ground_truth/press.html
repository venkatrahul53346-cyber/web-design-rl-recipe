Press Contact

Write to Wren.

Our director of communications handles all editorial requests, photography licensing, and on-site visits. For private events or general inquiries, please use the addresses in the footer.

We do not host food bloggers or unsolicited tastings during regular service. Working-press visits are arranged Thursday and Friday only, with two weeks’ notice.
