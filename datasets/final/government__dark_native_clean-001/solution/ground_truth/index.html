§ 05 — Procedure

How to request a record

Full guidance →
STEP 01

Find the reference

Use the catalogue to locate the file. Every item has a unique reference (e.g. CAB/14/229) that you will need on the request form.

STEP 02

Check access status

Most records are open. Files marked restricted, embargoed, or sensitive require either a justified request or a wait for the embargo to lapse.

STEP 03

Submit the form

Open records can be ordered for the reading room or as a reproduction. Restricted records require an FOI form and, where applicable, a stated public-interest justification.

STEP 04

Receive a determination

Open requests are typically fulfilled within 5 working days. FOI determinations are issued within 20 working days under the Records Act.

For researchers & journalists

Register for a reader's ticket

A reader's ticket grants in-person access to the reading rooms and the ability to order original material from secure storage. Free of charge, valid for three years.
