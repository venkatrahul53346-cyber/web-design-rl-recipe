How a request is handled

Four steps, from submission to release

Step 01

Submit

You complete the form online or in person. A reference number is issued within one working day.

Step 02

Assess

A registrar locates the file and reviews it against access, privacy, and exemption rules.

Step 03

Decide

You receive a written decision: full release, partial release with redactions, or refusal with reasons.

Step 04

Deliver

Records are made available digitally, in the reading room, or as a reproduction, per your preference.
