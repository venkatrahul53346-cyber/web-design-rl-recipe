FEATURED 2025-03-12 RELEASES REF NA/2025/0341

Cabinet papers from 1995 released after thirty-year rule embargo

The Archive has published 18,402 Cabinet minutes, memoranda, and ministerial correspondence covering the calendar year 1995. The release includes 1,210 files previously withheld under sections 23 and 27 of the Public Records Act, now reviewed and opened in full. Files are searchable through the online catalogue and viewable in the Westminster reading room.
