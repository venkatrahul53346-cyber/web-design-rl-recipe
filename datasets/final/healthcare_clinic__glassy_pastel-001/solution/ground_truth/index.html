Why academic matters

The treatment you get on Tuesday is the treatment we'll teach on Friday.

As a teaching hospital affiliated with Greenfield University College of Medicine, our clinicians train the next generation while running 42 active trials in oncology, cardiology, and neurodegenerative disease. Patients have direct access to therapies years before they reach community practice.
