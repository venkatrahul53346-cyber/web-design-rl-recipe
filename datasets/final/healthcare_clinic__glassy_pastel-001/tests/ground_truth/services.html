What to expect

Your pathway through specialty care.

Every specialty visit follows the same predictable arc — so you know what's next, who you'll meet, and when you'll hear back.

Step 01

Request or referral

Self-refer online, or have your primary care clinician send records securely. We confirm within 2 business days.

Step 02

Triage & matching

A nurse navigator reviews your case and matches you with the right subspecialist and care setting.

Step 03

Consultation

Your first visit reviews history, imaging, and prior workup. Most visits include same-day labs or imaging.

Step 04

Plan & follow-through

You leave with a written plan in the portal, scheduled follow-up, and a direct line to your care team.

For referring clinicians

A direct line into every institute.

The Cardinal Physician Liaison Service connects community providers with our specialists for curbside consults, expedited transfers, and shared-decision review on complex cases — without losing the patient relationship you've built.

Call physician line · 1-800-555-0155 Secure referral form

What we send back

  • Consultation note within 24 hours of the visit
  • Direct EHR integration with Epic Care Everywhere
  • Shared imaging access through the regional PACS
  • Co-management plan with clear handback criteria
  • CME conferences open to community physicians
