Getting here

Transit, parking, and access

Every Cardinal site is wheelchair-accessible with assisted-listening devices available. Interpreter services for 60+ languages are free of charge.

Bus
RTA routes 4, 12, and the University Express stop at the main entrance.
Shuttle
Free inter-campus shuttle every 20 minutes, 6a–10p.
Rideshare
Dedicated pickup loop at the East entrance of the University Campus.
Parking
Validated garage parking for outpatients; valet available 7a–7p.
Accessibility
Step-free entrances, mobility loan programme, sensory-friendly waiting areas.
Not sure where to go?

Our care navigators will route you to the right specialist and site — usually within one business day.

Call 1-800-555-0140, weekdays 7a–7p. After hours, the 24-hour nurse line can help triage non-emergency concerns.
