Response promises

What happens after you hit send.

  1. An engineer reads it — same day

    Weekdays 08:00–20:00 CET and 09:00–18:00 ET. No autoresponder, no "thanks for reaching out" placeholder.

  2. We try the thing you described

    If your message includes a trace ID, repo, or runtime version, we'll reproduce locally before replying. Roughly 60% of first replies include a working patch or config.

  3. You stay in one thread

    No ticket numbers. No transfers between Tier 1 and Tier 2. The person who replies first owns it until it's resolved.

  4. We close the loop publicly

    If your issue uncovers a bug or doc gap, it ships to the changelog with a credit (if you want one).
