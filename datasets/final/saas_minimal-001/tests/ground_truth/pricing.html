Questions

Common pricing questions.

Anything else? Email hello@riverbed.dev — a founder will get back to you within a day.

What counts as a span?

One unit of work inside a trace — typically an HTTP handler, a database query, an outbound RPC, or a background job. A single user request usually produces 8 to 40 spans depending on how many services it crosses. We don't bill on logs, metrics, or seats.

What happens if I go over my included spans?

On Starter, we keep ingesting and email you when you hit 80% and 100%. You won't be billed; we'll just sample more aggressively until the next cycle. On Team, overage is metered at $0.18 / million with a hard-cap option in settings.

Do you charge per host or per agent?

No. Run the agent on a thousand pods or three — the agent is open source and free. We only price the spans that arrive at the hosted query plane.

Can I self-host the query plane too?

Yes. The OSS distribution includes the storage engine and query UI under Apache 2.0. Enterprise customers can also run the full hosted plane in their own VPC with our Helm chart and a license key.

Is there a discount for startups or non-profits?

Startups under 2 years old and $5M raised get the Team tier free for 12 months. Open-source maintainers and registered non-profits get 80% off forever. Apply from your billing settings after signup.

How do I cancel?

One button in billing settings. Your data stays queryable read-only for 30 days, then we delete it. No retention fee, no exit interview.
