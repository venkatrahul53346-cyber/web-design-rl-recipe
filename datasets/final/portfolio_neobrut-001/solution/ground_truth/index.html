§ 04 — How we work

A four-stage process. No discovery deck theatre, no retainers that out-live the founder.

We start with the verb, not the moodboard. Every page should do something a static comp can’t prove.

↳ 01 — Brief

Two weeks of impolite questions.

We interrogate the brand, the audience, the analytics, and — quietly — the org chart. The output is a one-page direction your CEO can read in an elevator.

↳ 02 — Direction

Art direction in motion, not in stills.

Two divergent routes, each shown as a 30-second prototype. You pick the one that doesn’t lie about what the site will feel like at 2 a.m. on a phone.

↳ 03 — Build

Next.js, GSAP, WebGL where it earns its weight.

Engineering and design share one Figma file and one repo. CMS is Sanity or Storyblok unless you have feelings about it.

↳ 04 — Launch & aftercare

We don’t disappear at deploy.

Six weeks of paid surveillance: performance budgets, motion regressions, analytics annotations on every release. After that we’ll happily train your in-house team or stay on a quarterly cadence — your call.

§ 05 — Capabilities

Five things we’re extremely good at, and a sixth we’re honest about.

No SEO retainers, no “growth hacking,” no AI-generated case studies. We make websites with a pulse and ship them on time.
