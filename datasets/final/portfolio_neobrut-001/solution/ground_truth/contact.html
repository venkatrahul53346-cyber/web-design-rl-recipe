Before you write — 04 / 04

Questions we
get a lot.

Read about the studio →

Do you work with startups?

Yes — when there’s a real product and a real story. We’ve launched five Series-A brands in the last two years, including a Montréal-based climate-tech and a Brooklyn fragrance house. We don’t do logo-on-Fiverr-budget work.

What does a project cost?

Single-page motion sites land around $45–80k CAD. Full-site rebuilds with custom WebGL run $120–280k. Flagship multi-market work is bigger. We send a tailored estimate within five business days of receiving your brief.

How long does it take?

A focused site: 8–12 weeks from kickoff. A cinematic flagship: 16–22 weeks. We protect timelines aggressively — no “Q1 became Q3” surprises. If your launch is fixed, tell us in the brief.

Can you white-label or sub-contract?

Occasionally, for agencies we already trust. Reach out through partners@ with the scope, the end client (under NDA is fine), and the build window. We say no more often than yes.

Do you ship code, or only design?

Both. The studio is half designers, half engineers — same room, same Slack, same daily stand-up. We hand over production-ready Next.js, Sanity / Contentful, and the WebGL layer on top.

What if we already have a design team?

Great — most of our best work is collaborative. We can plug in as the motion & build crew, working from your design system, your tokens, and your Figma. We won’t insist on owning the brand.
