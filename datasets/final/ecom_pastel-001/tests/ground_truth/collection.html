A note from the workshop

The Stoll CMS 330 we knit on was built in 1986. It still does the only thing we ask of it.

Each piece passes through fourteen pairs of hands before it leaves Vico Lungo Gelso. We've stopped counting how long it takes — we count the pieces instead.
