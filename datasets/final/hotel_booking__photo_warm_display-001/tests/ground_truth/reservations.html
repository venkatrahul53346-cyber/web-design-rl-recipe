House Notes

A few small things, in advance.

Children

Guests aged twelve and older are welcome at the long table; younger diners are happily hosted at private two-tops with advance notice.

Dress

There is no code. The walk from the parking field is gravel and the dining room runs cool — bring a layer between October and April.

Phones & photography

Quiet phones, no flash. We're happy to photograph the table for you between the third and fourth courses.

Accessibility

The dining room, restrooms and two of the seven rooms are step-free. Tell us in the notes and we'll make sure the path from the parking field is lit and graded.

Gift the dinner

Gift cards in any denomination ship by post within the week, tied with twine and a hand-written note from the kitchen.

Last-minute seats

Same-day openings — usually a returned two-top — are posted to our Instagram around 2 PM. First message wins the seat.
