Press & Awards

In the papers,
at the table.

Salt & Field opened in the spring of 2022 as a quiet experiment — a forty-seat dining room set inside a working hay barn in the Wallowa Valley. In the seasons since, writers, photographers and guidebook editors have made the drive. What follows is a record of their visits, along with the materials available to journalists planning their own.
